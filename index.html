Who's this chick?

I'm a daughter, a friend, a communicator, a traveller, and a student of life. I have seen the world, but it only makes me realize how far I have come short of what I can be.

Can you say you can write in three languages?

Consuming fine writings of the past and the present has set me up with an inquisitive rigor that inveigles a wish to mull over existential issues, such as identity, time and the best way to spend it. I write to tell a story; I write because there are ideas worth spreading; I write to be heard.
